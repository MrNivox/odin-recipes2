Beef with sauce on Macaronni

Nothing really to explain , its macaronni followed by beef and tomato sauce. Spices added like salt , garlic etc.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Macaronni
  • Tomato sauce
  • Salt
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook the Beef
  2. While its cooking add your spices
  3. When its cooked add your tomato sauce and reduced heat to a simmer
  4. aside from that , cook ur macaronni adding a pinch of salt
  5. when the macaronni are cooked remove the water keeping half a cup
  6. take the sauce off the heat and add it to your macaronni
