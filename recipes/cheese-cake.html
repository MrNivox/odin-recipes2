Cheese Cake

Cheese cake with raspberry sauce on top

Sweet cheese cake with raspberry sauce on top , with extra raspberies on it. With a cooki crumble bottom.

Ingredients

  • cream cheese
  • raspberyy sauce
  • raspberies
  • Cookies
  • vanilla
  • sugar
  • butter

Steps

  1. crumble the cookies and make a layer a the bottom of the pan
  2. melt the butter and pour it over the cookies
  3. then take your vanilla sugar and cream cheese and mix them togother
  4. then place over the cookies in a nice even layer
  5. bake it at 420 tile nice and golden brown
  6. once cooked let it cool for 20 mins
  7. then pour your raspberry sauce over it
  8. place a couple strawberries over it
